The Plonk It Guide to GeoGuessr

Recently added:

Mongolia, India, Kazakhstan, Nigeria and Mexico

Welcome to the Guide!

The Plonk It Guide to GeoGuessr was created to help players of almost all skill levels improve at the game.

To open a country page, click on the country on the map above or use the country list below.

This is a step-by-step guide, with each page consisting of three sections:

  • Identifying the country

  • Regional and subdivision-specific clues

  • Spotlight: very specific clues

Note: The guide only includes countries with official Google street view and their official coverage. Photospheres and Ari coverage won’t be included.

In each country, you will have numerous images of clues, accompanied by instructive descriptions. Hints beside the images give you a deeper explanation or draw comparisons to other countries.

To take a closer look at a clue, simply click on the image to get redirected to a Google Maps location showing it.
